My Story

Hey there! I wanted to share a bit about my journey with you. I'm Kolby Ellis, a marketing graduate from Grand Canyon University in Phoenix, Arizona, with a passion for creative problem-solving and innovation.

College: Nurturing Creativity in Marketing

My venture into the professional world began at Grand Canyon University in Phoenix, Arizona. As a marketing graduate, I honed my skills in creative problem-solving and innovation, setting the stage for an unexpected yet rewarding journey.

Post Grad: Entrepreneurial Ventures in Property Management

Post-college, I took a detour that caught many by surprise. For five fulfilling years, I spearheaded my own property management company in Phoenix, navigating the complexities of real estate with tenacity and passion.

Now: A Tech Enthusiast in the Making

Life has a way of steering you toward your true calling. In my case, it nudged me toward the dynamic world of technology. Since realizing my passion for tech, I've immersed myself in self-learning through platforms like Nucamp and Scrimba. July 2023 marked a pivotal moment as I proudly graduated from Nucamp with a certificate in Front End Web and Mobile Development.

Eager Learner, Ready for Challenges

Through diverse courses, I've acquired a toolbox of skills, delving into coding languages like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and more. My journey doesn't end here; I'm fueled by a perpetual desire to broaden my horizons in a real-world setting.

Quick Learner, Thriving in Team Dynamics

Whether collaborating in a team or tackling projects solo, I bring a quick learning mindset and a touch of perfectionism to the table. The prospect of working with seasoned professionals and exploring cutting-edge technologies is both thrilling and invigorating.

Entrepreneurial Spirit Meets Tech Savvy

Armed with an entrepreneurial background and a toolkit of self-taught technical skills, I'm not just seeking opportunities; I'm ready to contribute meaningfully to the ever-evolving landscape of technology.

Looking Forward to the Next Chapter

As I embark on the next chapter of my journey, I'm eager to face new challenges, embrace opportunities, and continue evolving. Here's to the unexpected and exciting twists that lie ahead!
